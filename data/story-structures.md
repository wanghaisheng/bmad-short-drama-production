# Story Structure Patterns

## Viral Short Drama Structure (Micro)
- **Opening (0-10s)**: High visual impact, immediate conflict, burning question.
- **Development (Middle)**: High information density, escalating conflict, at least 2 reversals/emotional beats.
- **Ending (Last 5s)**: Cliffhanger, suspense, or intense emotional satisfaction (payoff).

## Vertical Series Macro Structure (100 Episodes)
- **Setup (Ep 1-10)**: Introduce premise, main conflict, key relationships. Ep 1-3 must hook.
- **Rising Action (Ep 11-40)**: Initial skirmishes, small victories/defeats, deepening mystery.
- **Midpoint Climax (Ep 41-50)**: A major event shifts the dynamic (e.g., identity revealed to one person).
- **Dark Night (Ep 51-80)**: The antagonist gains upper hand, protagonist suffers major setback.
- **Climax (Ep 81-95)**: Final confrontation, truth revealed to all, villain punished.
- **Resolution (Ep 96-100)**: Happy ending, wedding, or setup for Season 2.

## Three-Act Structure
- **Act 1 (25%)**: Setup, inciting incident
- **Act 2 (50%)**: Confrontation, complications
- **Act 3 (25%)**: Resolution
