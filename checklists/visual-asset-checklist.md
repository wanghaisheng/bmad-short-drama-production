# Visual Asset Checklist (Posters & Thumbnails)

Use this to ensure your promotional images get clicks.

## 1. Composition
- [ ] **Rule of Thirds**: are eyes on the upper third line?
- [ ] **Focal Point**: Is there one clear subject (usually a face)?
- [ ] **Depth**: Is the background separated from the foreground?

## 2. Emotional Signal
- [ ] **Expression**: Is the emotion extreme (lust, rage, fear, joy)?
- [ ] **Relationship**: Is the dynamic between characters clear (lovers, enemies)?
- [ ] **Tension**: Is there physical proximity or contact?

## 3. Typography (Title Design)
- [ ] **Readability**: Is the title readable at 1 inch size?
- [ ] **Contrast**: Does the text stand out against the background?
- [ ] **Keywords**: Are the genre keywords (CEO, Revenge, Baby) prominent?

## 4. Technical Specs
- [ ] **Resolution**: Is it high enough for 9:16 displays?
- [ ] **Safe Zones**: Is important content away from UI elements (like/comment buttons)?
