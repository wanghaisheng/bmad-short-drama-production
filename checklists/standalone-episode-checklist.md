# Standalone Episode Checklist

## Hook (First 3 Seconds)
- [ ] Visual impact (close-up/action/high contrast)
- [ ] Sharp line or question
- [ ] Audio cue aligned to tension

## Conflict & Payoff
- [ ] Clear visible conflict
- [ ] Stakes escalate within 30–45 seconds
- [ ] Strong payoff or twist within 60–90 seconds

## Pacing
- [ ] No filler shots (establishing ≤ 1s)
- [ ] Information density ≥ 2 beats/min
- [ ] Dialogue punchiness score ≥ 7/10

## Visuals
- [ ] Subject/action/environment/lighting/camera defined
- [ ] Motion vocabulary applied (pan/tilt/dolly/zoom)
- [ ] Vertical composition respected (9:16)

## CTA & Shareability
- [ ] Clear CTA (follow/next episode/series tag)
- [ ] Memorable frame for preview
- [ ] Caption optimized for platform
