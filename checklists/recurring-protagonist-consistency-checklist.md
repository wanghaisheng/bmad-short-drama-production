# Recurring Protagonist Consistency Checklist

## Identity Consistency
- [ ] Character references (appearance/clothing) defined
- [ ] Visual tags reused across episodes
- [ ] No drift in core traits or status

## Arc Continuity
- [ ] Episode-to-episode goal progression tracked
- [ ] Reversals/setbacks logged and visible
- [ ] Relationships evolve logically

## Dialogue & Performance
- [ ] Speech patterns consistent (tone/phrasing)
- [ ] Signature lines or gestures retained
- [ ] Emotional beats escalate over time

## Visual Continuity
- [ ] Locations/props continuity maintained
- [ ] Lighting style consistent per genre
- [ ] Camera language coherent across episodes

## QA Gates
- [ ] Hook strength maintained ≥ 8/10
- [ ] Conflict density ≥ 3 per episode
- [ ] Cliffhanger presence per episode
