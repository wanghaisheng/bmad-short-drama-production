# AI Video Prompt Checklist

Use this to ensure your prompts for Vidu/Sora/Runway are complete and executable.

## 1. Subject Definition
- [ ] **Character Consistency**: Are the character's key features (hair, clothes, age) repeated exactly?
- [ ] **Action**: Is the verb specific? (e.g., "running desperately" vs "running")
- [ ] **Emotion**: Is the facial expression clearly defined?

## 2. Environment & Lighting
- [ ] **Setting**: Is the background described? (e.g., "luxury penthouse, night")
- [ ] **Lighting**: Is the lighting style specified? (e.g., "cinematic lighting", "volumetric fog", "neon lights")
- [ ] **Weather/Atmosphere**: Is there rain, snow, or smoke?

## 3. Camera Control
- [ ] **Shot Type**: Is it a Close-up, Wide shot, or Medium shot?
- [ ] **Movement**: Is the camera moving? (e.g., "dolly in", "pan right", "handheld shake")
- [ ] **Angle**: Is it low angle (power) or high angle (vulnerability)?

## 4. Style & Technical
- [ ] **Aspect Ratio**: Is it `--ar 9:16` for vertical video?
- [ ] **Quality Tags**: Are tags like "8k", "photorealistic", "film grain" included?
- [ ] **Negative Prompts**: Are unwanted elements excluded? (e.g., "cartoon", "blur", "bad hands")
